How to
Play

01
Gather your people

Works best with 3–15 players. Strangers at a bar, friends at dinner, travelers at a hostel. Anyone, anywhere. The more different you are from each other, the better.

02
Shuffle and place the deck face down

Take turns drawing clockwise — or use the app to randomize who goes next. No cheating. No peeking. No skipping (unless you sip).

About

The Story

Only in Thailand was born in the streets of Bangkok. Created by Pub Crawls Thailand and Dinner With Strangers — two events that have spent years bringing travelers, expats, and locals together in one room and watching something real happen.

Why We Made It

We kept watching strangers sit next to each other at bars, at hostels, at dinner tables — and never talk. Not because they didn't want to. Just because nobody started. So we made the thing that starts it.

How It's Played

80 cards. 4 categories. No filters. Designed for the kind of night where you don't check your phone and you leave knowing at least five new things about the people you met. It's played on pub crawls, at dinner events, and around the world anywhere the energy is right.

Pub Crawls Thailand

Bangkok's biggest pub crawl experience. Every night brings together travelers, expats, and locals for nights that don't end until the sun comes up. This game is what happens between bars — when the music is too loud to talk but the conversation doesn't stop anyway.

Dinner With Strangers

A curated dining experience where strangers sit at the same table, share a meal, and leave as something more. Only in Thailand started as the icebreaker at our tables and grew into something that travels the world in people's bags.
